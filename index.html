Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is focusing on getting the content to the audience while not focusing on fancy and complicated mechanics and designs. Progressive Enhancement makes sure that any browser will be able to get the information that the site is trying to provide. This is important in modern web development because of the amount of users with different preferences. Some people may get confused from the fancy designs and functions a site might have. Others may not be as technologically inclined and want the information in an easy to find area. Then there may also be people who like the extra functions and designs a site can have. Progressive Enhancement allows both extremes to be satisfied, by making the content easy to find, but also allowing designs to be present. More information can be found here.
